NipTuck Demo

Interactive setup guide – follow the steps below

1

Welcome

This demo scans you with the camera and gives a blunt, honest cosmetic assessment – like a comedian consultant. No filters, no sugarcoating.

2

Equipment

You need:

  • A webcam (e.g. Logitech MX Brio) or built-in camera
  • Good lighting – face and upper body clearly visible
  • Browser permission for camera access when prompted
3

Positioning

Stand or sit so the camera sees you clearly:

  • Waist up – face, neck, shoulders visible
  • Or full body – head to feet in frame
  • Face the camera directly – no profile or blocked face
4

Scan

When the demo starts, a sci‑fi style scan overlay appears on the camera. Position yourself in frame, click Scan me. You’ll get an attractiveness score, celebrity lookalikes, and a blunt cosmetic assessment – spoken aloud.

5

Doc X video & discuss

Doc X video – A HeyGen avatar delivers your assessment on video (clinic view). Click to generate (takes ~30s).

Discuss with Doc X – Speak to ask questions. Doc X replies out loud.

Patient view – See yourself in scenarios (beach, gym, boudoir…) with body contouring. Full tutorial · Both views

6

Ready?

Click Start Demo below to begin. You can return to this guide anytime.
