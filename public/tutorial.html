Two Sides of the Experience

NipTuck works in two modes: the clinic view (doctor) and the home view (patient). Each sees a different angle of the same consultation.

👨‍⚕️ Doc View – Clinic Interior, One-on-One

Camera angle: Professional consultation setup. The doctor sees the patient on screen (or in person with a kiosk). Clean clinic interior.

HeyGen avatar (Doc X): A talking avatar delivers the blunt cosmetic assessment. The avatar speaks the patient’s score, celebrity lookalikes, and recommendations – like a comedian consultant.

Flow:

Patient scans Assessment generated Doc X video speaks it Discuss via mic
Try Doc View →

🏠 Patient View – At Home, Having Fun, Modeling

Camera angle: Home interior – living room, bedroom, wherever the patient is. Casual, fun, like trying on looks in the mirror.

The magic: Patient scans, then picks scenarios (beach, gym, red carpet, boudoir, action hero…). They see themselves transformed – body contouring, different settings, different vibes. It’s modeling and play.

Flow:

Scan in frame Pick scenario AI generates image/video See your look – magically
Try Patient View (scenarios) → Big Screen Demo →

Body Contouring – The “Magic”

Using your scan, the system applies professional body contouring to generated images. Same face, same you – but with subtle enhancements: slimmer silhouette, toned look, different hair, different setting.

It’s not a filter – it’s AI-generated imagery that keeps your likeness while showing “what could be” in different scenarios. Perfect for consultations and at-home try-on.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

1. Start the demo

Open demo.html or guide.html. Allow camera access. Position yourself waist-up or full body.

2. Scan

Click Scan me. The sci-fi overlay runs. You get: attractiveness score, celebrity lookalikes, blunt assessment (spoken aloud).

3. Doc side: Doc X video

Click ▶ Doc X video. A HeyGen avatar delivers your assessment on video (~30s). This is the “clinic consultation” moment – the avatar speaks for the doctor.

4. Patient side: See yourself in scenarios

Scroll to See yourself in different scenarios. Pick a card (beach, gym, action hero, boudoir…). Choose Image or Video. Your scan is transformed into that setting – body contouring applied, same you, new look.

5. Voice it

Say “Put me at the beach” or “I want to look like an action hero.” The system parses your intent and generates the matching scenario.
